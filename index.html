To increase the safety of our patients and staff during the COVID-19 pandemic, we are now offering parking lot check-in!

When you arrive at our office for your appointment, click on the “Parking Lot Check-in” button for your location. Enter in your name, your phone number, and then click add. This will notify our staff that you are here and allow you to wait in your vehicle. Once an exam room is available, you will receive a text message alert. Then, come inside and we will get into an exam room right away.
