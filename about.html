Dr. Stephen C. Verral and his family are happy to be relocating to the Adirondack region from Michigan. Dr. Verral's wife is originally from Niskayuna, NY and they have strong family ties in this area.

Dr. Stephen C. Verral was born and raised in Lambertville, Michigan and received his Bachelor of Arts from the University of Michigan in 1992. He received his Masters in Public Health from the University of Illinois and graduated with a medical degree from A.T. Still University in Kirksville, Missouri. He completed his specialty training in Dermatology in association with Michigan State University and Oakwood Southshore Medical Center where he served as Chief Resident in the Department of Dermatology.

A licensed physician in both New York and Michigan, Dr. Verral specializes in general, surgical and cosmetic dermatology. He offers comprehensive dermatologic care in a friendly, comfortable environment.
