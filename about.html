Stephen Verral, D.O., M.P.H.

Dr. Stephen C. Verral has been practicing dermatology in Glens Falls, NY for almost 10 years. He is married and has two boys who keep him busy traveling to musical performances and multiple sporting events. Dr. Verral was born and raised in Michigan and received his Bachelor of Arts from the University of Michigan. Go Blue! He received a Master in Public Health degree from the University of Illinois and graduated with his medical degree from A.T. Still University in Kirksville, Missouri. He completed his specialty training in Dermatology in association with Michigan State University and Oakwood Southshore Medical Center where he served as Chief Resident in the Department of Dermatology. He is a member of numerous professional societies and lectures both locally and nationally. A licensed physician in New York and Michigan, Dr. Verral is board certified in dermatology and specializes in general, surgical and cosmetic dermatology. He is a Fellow in both the American Academy of Dermatology and the American Osteopathic College of Dermatology.

Derek Richardson, M.D.

Dr. Derek Richardson has been practicing in Glens Falls, NY for over 30 years. He is married with 5 children and 3 grandchildren. After years of us asking, he joined Gateway Dermatology, P.C. in 2015. He brings years of experience and expertise to our excellent medical team. Derek Richardson graduated with his Bachelor of Science from Yale University. He received his medical degree from St. Louis Univeristy in St. Louis, Missouri. He completed his specialty training in Dermatology at Albany Medical Center. He is a member of numerous professional societies. A licensed physician in New York, Dr. Richardson is board certified in dermatology and specializes in general and surgical dermatology. He is a Fellow in the American Academy of Dermatology.

Tess Pollinger, D.O.

Dr. Tess Pollinger attended the University of Albany where she received a bachelor's degree in Biology. She completed her medical degree at New York College of Osteopathic Medicine. Dr. Pollinger completed her residency training at Walter Reed National Military Medical Center in Dermatology where she received a Certificate of Achievement for Exceptional Accomplishment. She was chief resident during her last year. Dr. Pollinger is a member of the American Society of Dermatologic Surgery and the American Academy of Dermatology.

Kelly Reed, RPA-C

Kelly Reed is a Certified Physician Assistant who has been specializing in Dermatology for over 12 years. She is married with two children and grew up in the Glens Falls area. She has been practicing at Gateway Dermatology since 2008. Since joining our team, she has been an asset bringing years of experience in dermatology. Prior to becoming a physician assistant Mrs. Reed was a Registered Nurse for 12 years. Kelly graduated from Albany Hudson Valley Physician Assistant program and holds an AAS degree in Physician Assistant Studies from Hudson Valley Community College and a Certificate in Physician Assistant Studies from Albany Medical College. She also holds a Masters degree in Physician Assistant Studies from A.T. Still University with a focus in education and leadership. Kelly is a fellow member of the American Academy of Physician Assistants and Society of Dermatology Physician Assistants. She is recognized by the American Academy of Dermatology as a DermCare Team member.

Eric Gold, RPA-C

Eric Gold has been practicing as a physician assistant for 14 years. He is married to a nurse and has three children that keep their parents on the go with travel and sports (especially baseball-one of Eric’s true loves!), orchestra and other various activities. Eric is from Schenectady and has been living in the Saratoga area for the past 12 years. Eric is a member of the The Society of Dermatology Physician Assistants, and The American Academy of Physician Assistants. Brittany Buza, RPA-C

Brittany Buza is a Certified Physician Assistant who has been practicing in the dermatology specialty since 2016. She earned her Bachelor of Science degree from Nazareth College in Rochester, NY. After completing two years of clinical hours in plastic surgery as a medical assistant, she went on to complete her Master’s degree in Physician Assistant studies at SUNY Upstate Medical University in Syracuse, NY. While performing clinical rotations in various specialties in the Utica, NY area, she discovered her passion for the dermatology specialty. Brittany enjoys all aspects of dermatology including medical, surgical and cosmetic procedures. Brittany is a member of the Society of Dermatology Physician Assistants. She and her husband enjoy spending time with their mini-Aussie pup Winnie.
